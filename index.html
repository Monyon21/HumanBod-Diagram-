BodyWise

Interactive body map + Teachable Machine — learn anatomy & better health habits

About the project

BodyWise helps students and general learners explore human anatomy and learn healthy habits. It combines a clickable body diagram with a Teachable Machine image model that recognizes body parts via webcam (Head/Face, Arms/Hands, Legs/feet, Eye, Ears).

Problem it solves

Many learners memorize parts without connecting them to function and health. This app shows functions, common issues, and prevention tips — making health education practical and memorable.
